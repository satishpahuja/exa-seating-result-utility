Privacy Policy for Exa – Result/Seating Utility

Important Disclaimer

Exa is an independent educational utility application. It is NOT an official application of the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), the Government of India, or any other government authority.

This app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or associated with any government entity in any manner.

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Exa – Result & Seating Utility, developed and published by Satish Pahuja (“we”, “our”, or “us”).

Exa is an offline utility application designed for use by schools, educators, and examination administrators to process CBSE-related files provided through official channels.

Information Collection and Use

Exa does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

The application processes:

  • CBSE result .txt files provided to schools through official CBSE channels
  • Exam Centre LOC PDF files for seating plan generation

All file processing is performed locally on the user’s device. No data is uploaded to any server or shared with any third party.

Generated reports (Excel files and PDFs) are saved only in the device’s Downloads folder and remain fully under the user’s control.

Permissions

The app may request access to device storage solely to allow users to select input files and save generated reports. These permissions are used strictly for core app functionality.

Third-Party Services

Exa does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking tools, or cloud services that collect user data.

Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for use by educators, school staff, and examination authorities. It does not target children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from children.

Data Security

Since Exa does not collect or transmit personal data, all information remains private and secure on the user’s device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
